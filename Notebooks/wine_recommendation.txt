# Wine & Food Pairing Recommendation

**Wine Recommendation:**  
[**Moscato d'Asti Rocche dei Manzoni**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Moscato+d'Asti+Rocche+dei+Manzoni)  
**Wine Recommendation:**

**Moscato d'Asti Rocche dei Manzoni**

**Key Details:**

* **Color:** White
* **Flavor Profile:** Sweet with notes of honey, apricot, and peach
* **Aroma:** Intense floral aromas of roses, jasmine, and elderflowers
* **Sweetness Level:** 4 (Demi-Sec)
* **Body:** Medium-bodied
* **ABV:** 5.5%
* **Price Range:** $15 - $25

**About the Wine:**

Rocche dei Manzoni Moscato d'Asti is a sparkling white wine from the Piedmont region of Italy. It is made from the Moscato Bianco grape variety, known for its intense sweetness and floral character. The wine is produced using an aromatic fermentation process that preserves the grape's delicate flavors and aromas.

This Moscato d'Asti exhibits a lively and effervescent nature, with a balanced sweetness and a medium body that provides a satisfying fullness on the palate. Its floral aromas and honeyed flavors make it an excellent choice as an aperitif or dessert wine. It pairs well with fruit salads, desserts, and light cheeses.

<details>
  <summary><strong>Recipe & Instructions</strong></summary>
  **Recipe: Peach Melba with Honey-Almond Sabayon**

This elegant dessert perfectly complements the sweet and fruity notes of Moscato d'Asti Rocche dei Manzoni, creating a harmonious pairing that will delight your palate.

**Ingredients:**

**For the Honey-Almond Sabayon:**
- 4 large egg yolks
- 1/2 cup honey
- 1/2 cup Moscato d'Asti Rocche dei Manzoni
- 1/4 cup chopped toasted almonds

**For the Peach Melba:**
- 4 ripe peaches, peeled and sliced
- 1/2 cup raspberry preserves
- 1 cup vanilla ice cream

**Shopping List:**

- Eggs (4 large)
- Honey (1/2 cup)
- Moscato d'Asti Rocche dei Manzoni (1/2 cup)
- Almonds (1/4 cup, toasted and chopped)
- Peaches (4 ripe)
- Raspberry preserves (1/2 cup)
- Vanilla ice cream (1 cup)

**Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions:**

**For the Honey-Almond Sabayon:**

1. In a medium saucepan, whisk together the egg yolks, honey, and Moscato d'Asti.
2. Cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until the mixture thickens and coats the back of a spoon, about 5 minutes.
3. Remove from heat and stir in the toasted almonds. Set aside.

**For the Peach Melba:**

1. In a shallow dish, arrange the sliced peaches.
2. Top with the raspberry preserves, spreading evenly.
3. Place a scoop of vanilla ice cream on top of the peaches.

**To Serve:**

1. Divide the warm honey-almond sabayon among the peach melbas.
2. Serve immediately and enjoy the delightful combination of sweet, fruity, and creamy flavors.

**Tips:**

* For a more intense flavor, use a darker honey, such as buckwheat honey.
* To add a touch of tartness, consider adding a few drops of lemon juice to the raspberry preserves before topping the peaches.
* For an elegant presentation, drizzle the peach melbas with a bit of honey or garnish with fresh raspberries.
</details>
