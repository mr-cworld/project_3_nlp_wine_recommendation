# Wine & Food Pairing Recommendation

**Wine Recommendation:**
[**Zinfandel**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Zinfandel)
**Recommended Wine Pairing for BBQ Ribs:**

**Zinfandel**

**Specific Wine Recommendation:**

* **Ravenswood Zinfandel Vintner's Blend**

**Key Details:**

* **Origin:** California, USA
* **Varietal:** Zinfandel
* **Alcohol Content:** 14.5% ABV
* **Tasting Notes:** Bold and juicy flavors of ripe blackberries, black cherries, and spice. A hint of oak adds structure and complexity.
* **Food Pairing:** Ideal companion for grilled and barbecued meats, especially those with a sweet and smoky glaze.

**Why Zinfandel is a Great Pairing:**

* Zinfandel's high acidity cuts through the richness of the ribs and cleanses the palate.
* Its fruity and spicy notes complement the savory and smoky flavors of the barbecue sauce.
* The moderate tannins in Zinfandel provide a balanced structure that supports the robust flavors of the meat.
* The wine's juiciness enhances the overall mouthfeel of the meal.

<details>
  <summary><strong>Recipe & Instructions</strong></summary>
  **Zinfandel-Braised Short Ribs**

This rich and flavorful dish is the perfect complement to a bold Zinfandel. The wine's fruity notes and spicy undertones pair beautifully with the tender beef and smoky barbecue sauce.

**Ingredients:**

* 4 pounds beef short ribs
* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 1 large onion, chopped
* 2 carrots, chopped
* 2 celery stalks, chopped
* 2 cups Zinfandel wine
* 2 cups beef broth
* 1 cup barbecue sauce
* 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
* 1 tablespoon brown sugar
* 1 teaspoon smoked paprika
* 1 teaspoon dried oregano
* 1 teaspoon dried thyme
* Salt and pepper to taste

**Shopping List:**

* Beef short ribs
* Olive oil
* Onion
* Carrots
* Celery
* Zinfandel wine
* Beef broth
* Barbecue sauce
* Worcestershire sauce
* Brown sugar
* Smoked paprika
* Dried oregano
* Dried thyme
* Salt
* Pepper

**Cooking Instructions:**

1. Preheat oven to 325°F (163°C).
2. In a large Dutch oven or oven-safe pot, heat olive oil over medium heat.
3. Season short ribs with salt and pepper and brown on all sides.
4. Remove short ribs from pot and add onion, carrots, and celery. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
5. Pour in Zinfandel wine and bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer until the wine is reduced by half, about 10 minutes.
6. Add beef broth, barbecue sauce, Worcestershire sauce, brown sugar, smoked paprika, oregano, thyme, and season with additional salt and pepper to taste.
7. Return short ribs to the pot and nestle them in the sauce.
8. Cover and braise in the preheated oven for 2-3 hours, or until the meat is fall-off-the-bone tender.
9. Serve the short ribs over mashed potatoes or rice, spooning the braising liquid over the top.

Enjoy this delicious Zinfandel-Braised Short Ribs with a glass of your favorite Zinfandel!
</details>
