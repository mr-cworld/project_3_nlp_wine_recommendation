# Wine & Food Pairing Recommendation

**Wine Recommendation:**  
[**Key Details:**](https://www.google.com/search?q=Key+Details:)  
**Recommendation:** **Kim Crawford Sauvignon Blanc Marlborough**

**Key Details:**

* Wine Color: White
* Flavor Profile: Bright tropical fruits like passion fruit, pineapple, and mango
* Aroma: Crisp Granny Smith apple, citrus zest, and floral notes
* Sweetness Level: 7 (Semi-sweet)
* Body: Light-bodied
* Price Range: $15 - $30

This Sauvignon Blanc from New Zealand's celebrated Marlborough region perfectly matches the desired attributes. Its tropical fruit flavors, crisp acidity, light body, and affordability make it an excellent choice.

  ## Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Herb Butter

This grilled salmon dish perfectly complements the bright acidity and citrus notes of Key Details Sauvignon Blanc. The lemony herb butter enhances the fish's flavor, creating a tantalizing culinary experience.

### Ingredients:

#### Salmon:
- 4 salmon fillets (6 ounces each)
- Salt and black pepper to taste

#### Lemon-Herb Butter:
- 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter, softened
- 1 tablespoon lemon zest
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
- 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
- Salt and black pepper to taste

### Step-by-Step Cooking Instructions:

1. **Prepare the lemon-herb butter:** In a small bowl, combine the softened butter, lemon zest, thyme, rosemary, oregano, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined and set aside.
2. **Season the salmon:** Season the salmon fillets generously with salt and pepper on both sides.
3. **Grill the salmon:** Preheat a grill to medium-high heat. Brush the grill grates with oil. Place the salmon fillets on the grill and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until cooked through to your desired level of doneness.
4. **Top with lemon-herb butter:** Remove the salmon from the grill and immediately spread the lemon-herb butter evenly over the fillets.
5. **Serve:** Garnish with fresh herbs, such as parsley or chives, and serve with your favorite sides.
